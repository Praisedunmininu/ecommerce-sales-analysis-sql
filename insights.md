# E-Commerce Sales Insights

## 1. Regional Performance
- West region has the highest sales and profit
- East follows as the second-best performing region
- South and North show lower performance compared to West

---

## 2. Product Performance
- Electronics category generates the highest total sales
- Accessories also perform strongly with consistent demand
- Office category performs poorly due to only one product (Printer)

---

## 3. Product-Level Insights
- West region is driven by products like Monitor, Smartphone, and Mouse
- East shows strong demand for Smartphone, Keyboard, and Smartwatch
- South is heavily driven by Printer and Camera sales
- North shows balanced but lower-performing product distribution

---

## 4. Customer Behavior
- East region shows complementary buying behavior (smartphone + accessories)
- West shows diversified product demand across multiple categories

---

## 5. Quantity Analysis
- West has the highest quantity of items sold
- Differences in quantity across regions are small, suggesting other factors also influence performance

---

## 6. Average Order Value
- West has the highest average sales per order
- North has the lowest average order value
